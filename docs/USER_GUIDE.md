## For Judges

### Getting Started

**What is a judge?**  
As a judge, you've been invited by the competition organizer to evaluate projects. You'll score each project based on specific criteria and provide feedback to help teams improve.

### Logging In

1. Check your email for an invitation
2. Click the magic link in the email (no password needed!)
3. You'll be taken directly to your judge dashboard

💡 **Tip:** Can't find the email? Check your spam folder or ask the organizer to resend it.

### Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see:
- **Total Assigned**: How many projects you need to judge
- **Judged**: Projects you've completed
- **Remaining**: Projects still waiting for your scores
- **Completion**: Your overall progress

### Scoring a Project

1. Click on any project card to start scoring
2. You'll see 8 criteria to evaluate (each scored 1-10)
3. For each criterion:
   - **1-4**: Not good enough
   - **5-6**: Acceptable but needs work
   - **7-8**: Good work!
   - **9-10**: Excellent!

4. Add private notes (only you and organizers see these)
5. Write feedback for the team (they'll see this after judging ends)

### Saving Your Progress

- Click **"Save Progress"** anytime - your work is saved immediately
- You can come back and change scores before submitting
- Click **"Submit All Scores"** when you're done with all criteria

⚠️ **Important:** Once submitted, you can still review but major changes need organizer approval.

### Reading Project Materials

Each project may have:
- **PDF Document**: Their written proposal
- **Video Link**: A demo or pitch video
- **Team Details**: Information about who created it

### Keyboard Shortcuts

Want to score faster?
- Press **1-9** or **0** (for 10) to quickly score
- Press **Tab** to move to the next criterion
- Press **Shift+Tab** to go back

## For Organizers

### What Does an Organizer Do?

As an organizer, you're running the show! You can:
- Add projects (teams or submissions)
- Invite judges
- Assign which judge reviews which project
- Monitor progress
- View final results

### Getting Started

1. Create an account or log in
2. Set up your competition details
3. Start adding projects and judges
4. Make assignments
5. Monitor progress
6. Export results when ready

### Managing Projects

**Adding Projects Manually:**
1. Go to **Projects** page
2. Click **"Add Project"**
3. Fill in:
   - Project name (required)
   - Category (e.g., country, age group)
   - PDF document (optional)
   - Video link (optional)
4. Click **"Add Project"**

**Bulk Import:**
1. Prepare a spreadsheet (CSV or Excel)
2. Include columns: `project_name`, `category`, `pdf_url`, `video_url`
3. Click **"Import CSV"** or **"Import Excel"**
4. Upload your file - done!

💡 **Tip:** Download the sample file to see the correct format.

### Managing Judges

**Inviting Judges:**
1. Go to **Judges** page
2. Click **"Invite Judge"**
3. Enter their name and email
4. They'll receive a magic link to log in

**Managing Judge Status:**
- Toggle **Active/Inactive** if someone can't participate
- View their progress at a glance
- Send reminder emails if needed

### Making Assignments

**What are assignments?**  
An assignment means "Judge A will score Project B"

**How to assign:**
1. Go to **Assignments** page
2. Select a judge
3. Select a project
4. Click **"Create Assignment"**

💡 **Tip:** Each project should have 2-3 judges for fair scoring.

### Monitoring Progress

The **Dashboard** shows you:
- How many judges have started
- How many projects are fully judged
- Who's behind schedule
- Real-time completion percentages

### Viewing Results

1. Go to **Results** page
2. See all projects ranked by average score
3. Click any project to see individual judge scores
4. Export to CSV for deeper analysis

### Exporting Data

You can export:
- **All scores** - Complete judging data
- **Results summary** - Rankings and averages
- **Audit log** - Who changed what and when

## For Voters

### What is Public Voting?

Public voting (also called "People's Choice") lets anyone support their favorite projects. Your votes help determine part of the final score!

### How to Vote

1. Visit the voting page (no login needed!)
2. Browse all projects
3. Click **"Vote for this project"** on up to 3 projects
4. That's it - your votes are counted!

### Finding Projects

- **Search**: Type keywords to find specific projects
- **Filter**: Select a category to narrow down
- **Browse**: Scroll through all projects

### Vote Limits

- Most competitions allow **3 votes per person**
- You can change your votes before voting closes
- Click a voted project again to remove your vote

💡 **Tip:** Votes are stored in your browser. Clear your browser data and you'll need to vote again!

## Helpful Tips

### For Everyone

**Browser Compatibility:**
- Works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- Use a desktop or tablet for the best experience
- Mobile works too!

**Getting Help:**
- Look for the **?** help icon throughout the platform
- Contact your competition organizer
- Check the email you received for organizer contact details

### For Judges

**Best Practices:**
- Read the full project before scoring
- Watch videos if provided
- Be consistent - use the same standards for all projects
- Write constructive feedback - teams learn from it
- Take breaks - judging requires focus

**What if I know a team?**
- Click **"Flag Conflict of Interest"** on that project
- The organizer will be notified
- They may reassign it to another judge

### For Organizers

**Planning Your Timeline:**
- Give judges at least 5-7 days to complete scoring
- Send reminder emails 2 days before the deadline
- Plan results announcement 2-3 days after judging closes

**Ensuring Quality:**
- Assign 2-3 judges per project for balanced scores
- Check for conflicts of interest
- Review audit logs if scores seem unusual

### For Voters

**Voting Strategies:**
- Read project descriptions carefully
- Watch videos if available
- Consider impact and innovation
- Vote for projects you truly believe in

## Common Questions

**Q: I forgot to save my scores. Are they lost?**  
A: No! The platform auto-saves as you score. Just make sure to click "Submit All Scores" when completely done.

**Q: Can I change my scores after submitting?**  
A: Yes, you can review and make small adjustments. Big changes need organizer approval.

**Q: Why can't I score anymore?**  
A: The judging deadline has passed. The form becomes read-only after the deadline.

**Q: My project materials won't load. What should I do?**  
A: Try refreshing the page. If that doesn't work, contact the organizer - they may need to re-upload the files.

**Q: Can I vote more than 3 times?**  
A: No, the system limits votes per person to ensure fairness (exact limit set by organizer).

**Q: I'm an organizer. Can I also be a judge?**  
A: Yes! Just log out of the organizer portal and log in with your judge email.
