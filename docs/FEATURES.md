## For Judges

### Smart Scoring System

**Weighted Criteria**  
Not all criteria are equal! The platform automatically calculates weighted scores based on what matters most for your competition.

**Live Score Preview**  
See how your scores affect the final total in real-time. A helpful sidebar shows:
- Current weighted total
- Contribution from each criterion
- Progress towards completion

**Flexible Scoring**  
- Score from 1-10 on each criterion
- Change your scores anytime before submitting
- Save progress and come back later
- Submit when all criteria are complete

### Keyboard Shortcuts

**Speed up your judging:**
- `1-9` or `0` for scores
- `Tab` to jump to next criterion
- `Shift+Tab` to go back
- Quick navigation through projects

### Rich Project Materials

**View Everything:**
- PDF proposals (read in-browser or download)
- Video presentations
- Team information
- Project metadata

**Multiple Viewing Options:**
- Open PDFs in new tab
- View in embedded reader
- Download for offline review

### Feedback System

**Two Types of Notes:**

1. **Private Notes** (only you and organizers see)
   - Personal observations
   - Scoring rationale
   - Questions or concerns

2. **Team Feedback** (shared with participants after judging)
   - Constructive criticism
   - Suggestions for improvement
   - Highlights of what they did well

**Structured Feedback Template:**  
The platform prompts you to include:
- Strengths
- Areas to improve
- Key recommendations

### Conflict of Interest

**Stay Fair:**
- Flag projects where you know the team
- Organizers are automatically notified
- Project may be reassigned
- Your integrity protected

### Progress Tracking

**Always Know Where You Stand:**
- Dashboard shows total assigned projects
- See what's judged vs. pending
- Completion percentage
- Deadline countdown
